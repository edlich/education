This is a short guide for a perfect evening education video conference.

1. The content should be interesting and relevant,
with stimulating material which is structured and encourages active participation.

2. It helps, if the teacher uses interactive platforms like breakout rooms or tools like MIRO.

3. The connection should be stable, because a clear and concise delivery is essential.

4. The video confernce should allow and promote open discussions and questions.

5. Make sure that the video confernce is seperated in different time blocks that are individually not long.

6. The video conference should provide various materials in addition to promote the learning.

7. The video confernce should be open for feedback and concerns of the participants.
