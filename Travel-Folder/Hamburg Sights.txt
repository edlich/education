Hamburg is a vibrant city with a mix of maritime charm, historic architecture, and modern attractions. Here are the **top 10 sights to see in Hamburg**:

1. **Speicherstadt & HafenCity**

   * The historic warehouse district (UNESCO World Heritage) with its red-brick buildings, canals, and bridges. HafenCity is the modern waterfront district next door.

2. **Elbphilharmonie**

   * Hamburg’s iconic concert hall. You can visit the plaza for panoramic views of the harbor, even if you don’t attend a concert.

3. **St. Michael’s Church (Michel)**

   * One of Hamburg’s most famous landmarks. Climb the tower for sweeping city views.

4. **Miniatur Wunderland**

   * The world’s largest model railway and miniature world—amazing for both kids and adults.

5. **Port of Hamburg / Landungsbrücken**

   * Take a harbor boat tour or stroll along the piers to see massive ships and historic docks.

6. **Reeperbahn & St. Pauli**

   * Famous nightlife street in Hamburg. Even if you’re not partying, it’s interesting for its music history (The Beatles!) and vibrant culture.

7. **Planten un Blomen**

   * Beautiful park with themed gardens, ponds, and a water-light show in the evenings during summer.

8. **Hamburg Rathaus (City Hall)**

   * Stunning neo-Renaissance building in the city center. Guided tours showcase its grand halls.

9. **Alster Lakes (Binnen- & Außenalster)**

   * Picturesque lakes in the heart of the city. Ideal for walking, boating, or enjoying a café by the water.

10. **International Maritime Museum**

    * Fascinating exhibits about Hamburg’s seafaring history, ship models, and naval artifacts.
